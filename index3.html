About Me

UI/UX Designer

Hello, let me introduce myself, Rani Dhever Hani, a 6th semester student in the Digital Business Study Program at the Indonesian University of Education, Tasikmalaya Regional Campus. I have a great passion for exploring new things, especially in developing user experiences (UI/UX) that are dynamic and in line with user needs. My design approach starts with conducting in-depth user research, identifying existing problems, formulating appropriate solutions, then implementing them in a prototype that can be tested. The final stage is to carry out trials to ensure the effectiveness and success of the design.

My main focus is finding innovative ideas that can benefit many people, and then transforming them into designs that are comfortable and easy to enjoy. With this approach, I strive to produce end results that not only solve existing problems, but also create valuable new opportunities for society at large..
